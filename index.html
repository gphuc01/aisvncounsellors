Mental Health

Mental health encompasses emotional, psychological, and social well-being, influencing cognition, perception, and behavior. It affects how we think, feel, and act, and helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. While one's mental state may be different from person to person at different times, there are still steps that we can take in order to help ourselves.

Approximately 66.7% of students surveyed hhave experienced some form of emotional problems in the past few weeks

58.3% of students agreed that their mental health state has a large impact on their quality of work

83.4% of students surveyed never reached out to really talk about these problems with someone else.

No matter who it is, there are all steps that we can take to reach out to the people around us. If you need someone to talk to, our qualified counsellors are always ready to schedule meetings with you in order to provide you with the help you needed.
