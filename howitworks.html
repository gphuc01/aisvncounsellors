Frequently-Asked Questions

Q: What is mental health?

A: Mental health refers to our emotional, pyschological, and social well-being. Mental health plays a huge role in affecting things like how we make choices and handle stress in our lives.

Q: When should I reach out for help?

A: No matter how severe your condition is, you can always reach out to help at any time. If anything in general is affect your ability to do well in school or enjoy life, feel free to reach out for help. With us, all you really have to do is fill out the scheduling form to book an appointment.

Q: What are some ways we improve our mental health?

A: Some of the most important things that can help us improve and maintain a good mental health and wellbeing includes things that like sleeping properly, excercising, and doing things that can help us calm down like talking with our friends or writing a diary.
